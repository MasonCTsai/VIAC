Misconceptions

Blindness or visual impairment is a condition that means that you cannot see well. This can affect one or both eyes. Did you know that when someone is “blind”, it doesn't mean that they see pitch black? Most people who are blind can see some light, or flashes of light. They can also tell if an area is lit or dark. Others are not able to see light at all, though this is less common.
